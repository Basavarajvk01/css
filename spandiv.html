BUSSINESS

An artist is a person engaged in an activity related to creating art, practicing the arts, or demonstrating an art.
The common usage in both everyday speech and academic discourse refers to a practitioner in the visual arts only.


Begin by introducing yourself with your name, medium, and some background information.
This can include where you were born, where you work, and when you first became interested in art.
Next, discuss any art training or schooling you had and degrees earned. If none, state you're self-taught.
